About us


I Machine Tools Corp., professional CNC machine tool company in Taiwan, is specialized in making various kinds of CNC machines and machine tools. Our team includes active and professional managers, who have much manufacturing and exporting experience. Our company has excellent relationships with many of Taiwanese manufactures and always ready to provide a large variety of top quality machines at competitive prices for different kinds of customers' needs. Moreover, we also offer the full range of service and solutions for specific requirements of our clients all around the globe. Nowadays Taiwan is known to be the fourth largest manufacturer of metal processing equipment in the world. It's technological and labor base is quite sufficient for producing outstanding quality machinery. High precision and stable quality of Taiwan-made machine tools combined with competitive prices make our products the best solution for all of our customers representing different industries. iMachine tools Co. is and experienced team of managers from different countries with a deep understanding of the world machine tools market trends. This allows us to say that we are a bridge assisting many foreign companies in all their production needs.
